Who we are

Nick Lewis Ltd is a small business comprising of myself Nick (funnily enough!) and my wife Tina. We currently undertake contracts ranging from 3 month gigs to longer term endeavours. We are in the process of looking at various ideas of how we can develop this business in the future. Our focus is always going to be on helping people and business lead better informed lives.
